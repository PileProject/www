ABOUT

MISSION & TARGETS

Our mission is to provide a gate to "STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics)" fields for children. Children can foster logical thinking power by creating programs.

WHO WE ARE

We are a team focused on making general programming education available to anyone. We present a very easy and effective method of learning to program.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to build a world in which "anyone can make programs".

WORK FEATURES

As easy as putting programming blocks on a tablet, users can make programs which control a robot and learn the basic programming structures!

OUR WORK

ANDROID

Users can learn to program with our Android Apps. Anyone can enjoy programming by putting some blocks on the touchscreen without typing any key. Our visual programming style makes programming intuitive.

SIMULATOR

If you do not have a robot, we also provide an interactive robot simulator for our visual programming system. The simulator runs on your own PC, so you can start your wonderful programming life immediately!

HARDWARE

Have your first programming experience with our affordable hardware. If the users feel directly the output of their programs, learning becomes much more pleasurable.

EDUCATION

We provide workshops where we use our products. The workshops presented so far were a great success! We plan to prepare an efficient teaching material based on these experiences and their feedbacks.
