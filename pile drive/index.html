ABOUT

MISSION & TARGETS

Our mission is to provide a gate to "STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics)" fields for children. Children can foster logical thinking power by creating programs.

WHO WE ARE

We are the team to make "programming" general education for all people. We introduce a very easy and effective method of learning programming.

OUR GOAL

Our goal is to realize a world in which "all people can make programs."

WORK FEATURES

Very Attractive! Easy to Make Programs! Execution Flow Visualizing!

OUR WORKS

ANDROID

Users can learn programming on our Android Apps. Everyone can enjoy programming putting some blocks on touch panel without typing a keyboard. Our visual programming style, makes programming much familiar.

SIMULATOR

let's learn programming with your own tablet and robot simulator on your pc.we provide a robot simulator for our visual programming system.you can learn programming without expensive robots.

HARDWARE

Experience your first programming provided by our affordable gadget. We are trying to teach the pleasure of programming to everyone,also we think it is necessary for users to feel directly the output of their programs.

EDUCATION

We provide workshops where we use products which use products and we plan to make an efficient teaching material based on these experiences and feedbacks.workshop has been already done greatly succeeded.
